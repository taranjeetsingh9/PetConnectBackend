System Overview

This demonstration showcases a blockchain-based pet adoption system that permanently records adoption transactions on a distributed ledger. Each adoption creates an immutable record that cannot be altered or deleted.

  • Security: Cryptographic encryption ensures data integrity
  • Decentralization: No single point of failure
